About Me

About Me

Mustafa Nail Bülbül was born in Esslingen. In his childhood before he could even read or write, he devoted his interest to building and creating different things. When his father bought a computer, he started dealing with it. At high school, he attended the natural science subjects, where he discovered his interest in programming. Because of that he began to teach himself programming with small Java applications. Finally, together with three friends, he developed a little game that was inspired by Mario Card.

In addition to his interest in programming, he was enthusiastic about economy. As part of the JUNIOR institute, he and his business course founded a company in which he was employed as the Deputy Managing Director and the Head of Finance & Controlling. During his time in the high school he realized that he wanted to study business informatics in order to reconcile his interests. Since he likes to practice the things he is learning, only the dual study came into question.

In October 2017 consequently he began with his dual degree program at the Finanz Informatik Solutions Plus GmbH in Fellbach. Currently he is in the sixth and last semester and is actively involved in customer projects and is volunteering as the JAV chairman. He is active in various areas of the company and is responsible for the design and optimization of processes, but also the development of applications in various technologies.

In addition to his studies, Mustafa is engaged in the development of iOS applications, ReactJS applications and Java applications. In his spare time he likes to go out with his friends and practice various sports. He also likes to read and spread his knowledge in different areas.

Fitness - Gym-Workout-Planer is designed to create intuitively your own Workouts. The Simple Project

The Simple Project is a web-application which is used to organize the projects, customers, employees of the company. It is possible to create projects and assign a customer. Also it is possible to assign the employees who are working at the project. Like Jira for every project there is a kanban board where tasks could be created or assigned to another team member. This project was developed by 7 developers including me. I worked at this project also as an Technical Consultant and was heavily involved in developing the frontend and the backend. Furthermore I supported the team at technical issues.
